Japaneses Curry

Description

some description here

Ingredients

  • 1 box of Japanese Curry Roux (should be around 230-250g)
  • 2.5 Tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 large yellow onion sliced into 1/3" slices
  • Kosher salt, to taste
  • 5 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1" knob ginger, grated
  • 1 small Fuji apple, peeled and coarsely grated
  • 1.5 lb boneless, skinless chicken thighs, cut into 1" cubes
  • 1.5 Tbsp honey
  • 1 Tbsp ketchup
  • 2 Tbsp soy sauce
  • 5 cups chicken stock
  • 2 large carrots, peeled and cut rangiri style
  • 3 Yukon gold potatoes, peeled and quartered
  • Splash shiro dashi
  • 8 cups of cooked short grain rice, to serve
  • (optional) Japanese pickled vegetables (fukujinzuke)

Steps

  1. Heat up oil in medium sized pot. Medium to high heat.
  2. Add yellow onion.
  3. Add salt, garlic, ginger, and the small fuji apple. Stir and let it set for 10 seconds
  4. Add the Chicken.
  5. Add the honey, soy sauce, and ketchup. Mix it up.
  6. Add the chicken stock.
  7. Stir and then add the carrots and potatoes.
  8. Splash of shiro dashi (optional).
  9. Cook on medium-low heat for 15 minutes or until the potatoes are soft.
  10. Add the curry roux one square at a time dissolving by scooping broth and stirring it into it.
  11. After cook and stir for 2 mintues and then serve with rice.
