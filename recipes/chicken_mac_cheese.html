Crispy Chicken Mac & Cheese

Description

Do you like hot pasta and protein?!

Pasta sauce mixed with flavourful chicken.

High protein for those sick gains.

Anyone trying to make healthier choices will love this!

Ingredients

makes 5

For the Chicken

  • 900g boneless skinless chicken thighs
  • 2 tsp onion powder
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • 2 tsp smoked paprika
  • 125g panko bread crumbs
  • 3 eggs
  • Spray Oil
  • Salt and Pepper

For the Mac & Cheese

  • 400g high protein pasta of choice
  • 375ml low fat milk
  • 100g chedder cheese
  • 75g low-fat mozzarella cheese
  • 4 slices low-fat cheese
  • 1 Tbsp dijon mustard
  • Salt and Pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 430°F.
  2. Bring a pot salted water to a boil and cook the pasta for 1 minute less than the packet instructions. Reserve 1/2 cup of pasta water.
  3. Place and mix spices into a bowl and then add the chicken and cover all surfaces.
  4. Beat eggs into a bowl and add panko breadcrumbs to a second bowl. Dip the chicken into the egg and press firmly into the breadcrumbs.
  5. Spray both sides of the chicken, place onto tray, and place in oven. Cook for 18-20 minutes. Flipping halfway through.
  6. In a large pan over medium heat and add the milk. Heat until hot then add all the cheeses and gently stir until fully mixed.
  7. Add drained pasta water and mix well.
  8. Add the pasta and stir until completey coated.
